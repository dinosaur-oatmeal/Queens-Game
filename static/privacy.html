Privacy Policy

This website is built by Will Maberry. Here's how your data is handled:

Cookies

We use one cookie to remember your selected puzzle difficulty. This cookie is:

  • Essential for gameplay functionality
  • Only stored in your browser on your local machine
  • Expires after 1 day
  • Not used for any tracking or analytics

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your game progress for the "save state" feature. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is only used for saving your progress and returning to it
